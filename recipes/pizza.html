Yuvraj's Cheesy Pizza

Cheese Burst Pizza filled with onion,capsicum and olives

The pizza has a crisp,light and chewy base with great flavor (no overpowering smell of yeast!!).
The sauce is amazingly delicious and aromatic. Below is the full instructions from scratch-making
the pizza dough,making the sauce and assembling the pizza.

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • FLour
  • Sugar
  • Luke Warm Water
  • Tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup Oil
  • 2 chopped garlic cloves
  • Red chilli flakes

Steps

  1. Prepare the pizza dough
  2. Prepare the pizza base
  3. Chop all the vegetables for the pizza
  4. Spread the sauce and veggies on the base
  5. Bake the pizza at 250 degree Celsius for 10 minutes
  6. Sprinkle oregano and chilli flakes as per your taste
    7. Serve hot!!!!!!
